Overview

Conquer Spiti’s snow-covered terrains in a thrilling 4x4 expedition. This 7-day, 6-night adventure takes you through the most scenic winter routes of India, offering a blend of high-altitude scenic drives, ancient monasteries, and unique cultural experiences.

The expedition begins at Aut Tunnel and proceeds through the stunning landscapes of Jibhi, Chitkul, Tabo, Kaza, and Kalpa. You will experience local homestays, conquer challenging snow drives, and visit some of the world's highest settlements, including Komic, the highest motorable village, and Hikkim, home to the world’s highest post office.

Detailed Itinerary (7 Days)

Day 1: Arrival at Aut Tunnel | Transfer to Jibhi

Pickup from Aut Tunnel. Scenic drive to Jibhi. Evening bonfire & dinner at the stay. Stay: Jibhi.

Day 2: Jibhi to Chitkul / Sangla (The Last Village of India)

Breakfast & departure for Chitkul / Sangla. Drive through snow-covered valleys and mountain passes. Evening exploration at the Indo–Tibet border village. Stay: Chitkul / Sangla | Meals: Dinner.

Day 3: Chitkul / Sangla to Tabo

Drive along Sutlej & Spiti Rivers via Karcham and Pooh. Visit Nako Lake (weather permitting). Explore Tabo Monastery – over 1000 years old. Stay: Tabo | Meals: Dinner.

Day 4: Tabo to Kaza (via Dhankar Monastery)

Visit ancient Dhankar Monastery. Enjoy views at the confluence of Spiti and Pin Rivers. Arrive in Kaza by evening. Stay: Kaza | Meals: Dinner.

Day 5: Kaza Local Sightseeing (Komic – Hikkim – Langza Circuit)

Explore the high-altitude villages: Komic (World’s highest motorable village), Hikkim (World’s highest post office), and Langza (The fossil village with a Buddha statue). Stay: Kaza | Meals: Breakfast & Dinner.

Day 6: Kaza to Kalpa (via Chicham Bridge)

Morning drive via Asia’s Highest Suspension Bridge – Chicham. Reach Kalpa by evening; enjoy sunset over Mt. Kinner Kailash. Stay: Kalpa | Meals: Dinner.

Day 7: Kalpa to Jibhi / Tirthan – Drop at Aut Tunnel

Breakfast & drive back (Approx. 240 km / 8–9 hrs). Lunch en route. Evening arrival at Aut Tunnel – end of expedition. Ending Point: Aut Tunnel, Mandi.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Spiti 4x4 Snow Expedition safe in winter?

Safety is our top priority. The expedition is conducted using specialized 4x4 vehicles with experienced drivers who are experts in navigating snow and icy terrain. We carry support vehicles, medical kits, and emergency oxygen cylinders.

What kind of accommodation is provided?

Accommodation will be in hotels and guesthouses/homestays, which are chosen for their safety, warmth, and hospitality. Given the remote locations, these are generally basic but comfortable and authentic high-altitude stays.

Is there a lot of trekking or walking involved?

This is primarily a 4x4 road expedition, not a trek. Walking is limited to local sightseeing in villages like Komic, Hikkim, Langza, and visits to monasteries like Dhankar and Tabo. These walks are usually short and optional.

What is the typical group size?

We target a maximum group size of 40 people to ensure personalized attention and efficient logistics. Groups are divided among multiple 4x4 vehicles.
