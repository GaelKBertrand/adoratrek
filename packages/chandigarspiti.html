01

CHANDIGARH - RAMPUR

Meals: Dinner

We start from Chandigarh around 10 am for Rampur via Shimla & Narkanda. Check in at the property, unwind and we gather for an ice breaking session.

Highlights: Ice breaking session, Dinner.

02

RAMPUR - CHITKUL - RAKSHAM - SANGLA

Stay: Sangla | Meals: Breakfast & Dinner

We start after breakfast towards Chitkul via Sangla. On the way we cross the rock tunnel of Kinnaur. Reach Chitkul by Lunch time, visit the Indo Tibetan Army check post, Chitkul village and reach Sangla by evening after a beautiful drive.

Highlights: Rock tunnel of Kinnaur, Chitkul Village, Bonfire.

03

SANGLA - NAKO - GUE - TABO

Stay: Tabo | Meals: Breakfast & Dinner

We start early after breakfast towards Tabo, we stop by at the frozen Nako Lake. Take a detour to reach the Gue Monastery where we find a mummy of a Lama naturally preserved. Officially we enter Spiti Valley.

Highlights: Frozen Nako Lake, Gue Monastery Mummy, Star Gazing.

04

TABO - DHANKAR - PIN VALLEY - KAZA

Stay: Kaza | Meals: Breakfast & Dinner

We visit the Tabo caves and Tabo monastery. After breakfast we leave towards Kaza. On the way we visit the beautiful Dhankar monastery and Pin valley. We also do a stint of Off roading by the riverside. Reach Kaza and explore the market area and cafes.

Highlights: Tabo Caves, Dhankar Monastery, Pin Valley, Off-roading.

05

KAZA SIGHTSEEINGS

Stay: Kaza | Meals: Breakfast & Dinner

After breakfast start off with the Spiti Sightseeings. Langza Buddha statue, Komic , Hikkim, Key monastery, Kibber and Chicham Bridge.

Highlights: Langza Buddha Statue, World's highest post office (Hikkim), Key Monastery, Chicham Bridge.

06

KAZA TO KALPA

Stay: Kalpa | Meals: Breakfast & Dinner

Start early after breakfast for Kalpa. We spent some time at the Khab Sangam Bridge where the Spiti and the Sutlej rivers meet. Reach Kalpa by evening.

Highlights: Khab Sangam Bridge (Confluence of Spiti & Sutlej rivers).

07

KALPA - SARAHAN - NARKANDA

Stay: Narkanda | Meals: Breakfast & Dinner

Start early after breakfast and visit the Roghi view point in Kalpa. Shop at the Rekong peo market, a brief halt at the famous Bhima kali temple at Sarahan. reach Narkanda by evening.

Highlights: Roghi View Point, Rekong Peo Market, Bhima Kali Temple.

08

NARKANDA TO CHANDIGARH

Ending Point: Chandigarh

Start after breakfast and onwards to reach Chandigarh by 5pm and trip ends with happy unforgettable memories and new travel buddies for life.

Highlights: Drive back to Chandigarh.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Spiti 4x4 Snow Expedition safe in winter?

Safety is our top priority. The expedition is conducted using specialized 4x4 vehicles with experienced drivers who are experts in navigating snow and icy terrain. We carry support vehicles, medical kits, and emergency oxygen cylinders.

What kind of accommodation is provided?

Accommodation will be in hotels and guesthouses/homestays, which are chosen for their safety, warmth, and hospitality. Given the remote locations, these are generally basic but comfortable and authentic high-altitude stays.

Is there a lot of trekking or walking involved?

This is primarily a 4x4 road expedition, not a trek. Walking is limited to local sightseeing in villages like Komic, Hikkim, Langza, and visits to monasteries like Dhankar and Tabo. These walks are usually short and optional.

What is the typical group size?

We target a maximum group size of 40 people to ensure personalized attention and efficient logistics. Groups are divided among multiple 4x4 vehicles.
