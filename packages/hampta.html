Hampta Pass Trek

A classic 5-day trek from Manali into the Spiti Valley across the scenic Hampta Pass.
Himachal Pradesh
Return to list of treks
01

Manali → Jobra → trek to Chika (2,960 m)

Altitude: 2,960 m | Trek Distance: ~2 km

Arrive in Manali, drive to Jobra, and then begin a short trek to Chika campsite.

Highlights: Scenic drive, easy initial trek, setup camp by the river.

02

Chika → Balu Ka Ghera (3,150 m)

Altitude: 3,150 m | Trek Distance: ~6 km

Trek through beautiful meadows and across streams to reach Balu Ka Ghera, the "bear's abode."

Highlights: Picturesque landscapes, gentle ascent, diverse flora and fauna.

03

Balu Ka Ghera → Hampta Pass (4,270 m) → Shea Goru (3,700 m)

Altitude: Hampta Pass 4,270 m, Shea Goru 3,700 m | Trek Distance: ~10 km

The challenging pass crossing day. Ascend to Hampta Pass, enjoy panoramic views, and descend to Shea Goru campsite.

Highlights: Crossing Hampta Pass, stunning views of Pir Panjal and Spiti ranges, contrasting landscapes.

04

Shea Goru → Chatru → drive → Chandratal Lake (4,300 m) (subject to road condition)

Altitude: Chandratal Lake 4,300 m | Trek Distance: ~7 km trek + drive

Descend from Shea Goru to Chatru, then take a scenic drive to the mesmerizing Chandratal Lake. If road conditions are unfavorable, return directly to Manali from Chatru.

Highlights: Descent into Spiti Valley, stunning Chandratal Lake (if accessible), barren landscapes of Lahaul and Spiti.

05

Chandratal → Chatru → drive → Manali

From Chandratal, drive back to Chatru and then continue the scenic drive to Manali, marking the end of the trek.

Highlights: Return journey through the majestic Rohtang Pass (if open), reflecting on the trek's memories.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to do the Hampta Pass Trek?

The best time for the Hampta Pass Trek is from mid-June to mid-October. During this period, the weather is generally pleasant, and the pass is open and accessible.

What is the difficulty level of the Hampta Pass Trek?

The Hampta Pass Trek is rated as moderate (6/10). While it involves some steep ascents and descents, particularly on the pass day, it is manageable for individuals with good physical fitness and some prior trekking experience.

What kind of accommodation is provided during the trek?

During the Hampta Pass trek, accommodation is primarily in tents at campsites, offering an authentic outdoor experience. All camping gear, including sleeping bags and mats, are provided.

Is acclimatization necessary for this trek?

Yes, proper acclimatization is crucial. The itinerary is designed to allow for gradual ascent, helping your body adjust to the increasing altitude. Hydration and listening to your guide are key.

What essentials should I pack for the Hampta Pass Trek?

You should pack warm layers, waterproof and windproof jackets/pants, sturdy trekking shoes, a headlamp, personal medications, toiletries, sunglasses, and sun protection. A detailed packing list will be provided.

What is the maximum altitude reached on this trek?

The highest point on the Hampta Pass Trek is Hampta Pass itself, at an altitude of approximately 4,270 meters (14,010 feet). Chandratal Lake is at around 4,300 meters (14,100 feet).

Are there toilet facilities available on the trek?

At campsites, we set up hygienic dry pit toilets with toilet tents for privacy. It's important to use these facilities to maintain cleanliness on the trail.

Is it safe to drink stream water during the trek?

While most mountain streams have clean water, it is always recommended to use a water filter or purification tablets/drops for drinking water to avoid any stomach issues.

What if there is a medical emergency?

Our trek leaders are trained in wilderness first aid and carry basic medical kits. In case of serious emergencies, we have protocols for evacuation, which may involve porters or, in severe cases, helicopter rescue (at additional cost).

Can I do this trek if I am a beginner?

While Hampta Pass is considered moderate, beginners with good physical fitness and a commitment to regular pre-trek exercise can complete it. It's an excellent trek for those looking to upgrade from easy to moderate level.
